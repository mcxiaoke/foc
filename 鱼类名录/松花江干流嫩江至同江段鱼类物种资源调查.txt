松花江干流嫩江至同江段鱼类物种资源调查
赵帅，赵文阁，刘鹏
（哈尔滨师范大学生命科学与技术学院，哈尔滨150025）
摘要：为了解松花江鱼类种类组成的现状及变化趋势，笔者于2010年5—10月，通过走访当地居民、农
贸市场调查、标本采集等形式对松花江干流嫩江至同江段鱼类资源进行调查研究。结果表明，调查区域
共发现鱼类69种，隶属于2纲8目18科，占黑龙江省鱼类种类的63.9%，其中鲤科鱼类的种数最多，计有
35种，占50.7%，多为重要的经济鱼类；其次为鳅科，计有7种，占10.2%；鲑科和鲿科各4种，各占5.8%，
其他鱼类14科19种，占27.5%。调查结果显示，松花江干流嫩江至同江段的鱼类资源有减少的趋势，并
提出了相应的保护对策。
关键词：松花江；鱼类资源；保护

A Research of Songhua River Fish Species Resource from Nenjiang Section to Tongjiang Section
Zhao Shuai, Zhao Wenge, Liu Peng
(College 倀䜠Life 㐀䐀䨀䘀伀䐀䘠and 㔀䘀䐀䤀伀倀䴀倀䠀娀ഠHarbin ⼀倀匀一䈀䴠University, Harbin150025, Heilongjiang, China)
Abstract: In order to understand current situation and development trend of the Songhua river fish species
composition, the fish resources in the Songhua River were investigated based on interviewing with local
residents, trade market investigation and sample collection from May to October in2010. It is found that the
total number of fish species was69in the survey area, belonging to2classes,8orders,18families, accounting
for63.9% of Heilongjiang Province’s fish species.Cyprinidaehad the largest species (35), accounting for
about50.7% of the total number.Cobitidaehad the next largest species (7), accounting for about10.2% of the
total. BothAslmonidsandBagridaehad4species, accounting for5.8% of the total. The other19species
accounting for27.5% belonged to another14families. According to the results of the survey, the fish resources
had a decreasing trend, compared with the Songhua River from the Nenjiang section to the Tongjiang section,
and relevant suggestions were proposed for this phenomenon.
Key words: Songhua River; Fish Resources; Protection
